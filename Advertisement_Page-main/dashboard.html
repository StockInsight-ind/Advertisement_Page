❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add more than 3 stocks?
Upgrade to Pro to track unlimited stocks across all markets.
When are digests sent?
Digests are sent at 8 AM in your selected timezone based on your chosen frequency (daily/weekly/monthly).
What stock symbols should I use?
Use Yahoo Finance symbols. For NSE India add .NS (e.g. RELIANCE.NS), for BSE add .BO. US stocks use plain symbols (AAPL, TSLA).
Can I pause my subscription?
Yes! Go to Settings → Danger Zone and click "Pause Emails". You can resume anytime from your dashboard.
